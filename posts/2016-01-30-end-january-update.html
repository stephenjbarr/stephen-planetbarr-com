The book is organized to show progress of the field from logic based solvers to probabilistic constructs such as Markov logic networks (MLNs).

2 Probabilistic Soft Logic (PSL)

PSL is developed by Lise Getoor (formerly at UMD, now at UC Santa Cruz) and others. Like Markov Logic Networks, the language of PSL are logical atoms and statements. However, unlike MLNs, PSL was designed with efficient inference in mind, as the truth values are "soft", \(\in [0,1]\).
